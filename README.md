# Product Strategy Portfolio

I am a CFA-trained founder/operator who builds early-stage finance, technology, and alternative-asset products in complex or regulated markets.

I am strongest when an idea is promising but the path is unclear: the product, business model, legal/regulatory questions, capital markets logic, developer workflow, and operating plan all need to come together.

This is a product strategy portfolio, not a software engineering portfolio.

## Selected background

### Institutional product development

At Sun Life Financial’s International Investment Centre, I worked across product development, asset allocation, and due diligence / governance / manager research. The group managed 100+ global asset manager relationships and advised business units on external manager selection and investment product strategy.

### Brokerage and multi-asset markets

At TD Waterhouse, I supported high-value clients in a regulated brokerage environment across derivatives, foreign exchange, fixed income, equities, and bullion.

### Tokenized real-world assets

I helped create and launch First Carbon / MintCarbon, a climate-fintech platform focused on tokenizing voluntary carbon credits as ERC-1155 assets on Ethereum/Polygon.

The work included product architecture, on-chain/off-chain reconciliation, registry confirmation workflows, verifier roles, asset-claiming logic, retirement receipts, and securities/derivatives considerations.

### Founder/operator work

Through Charlotte Street Associates, I have advised and built with companies at the intersection of finance, alternative assets, fintech, blockchain/tokenization, and product strategy.

Selected project experience includes First Carbon, DeepMarkit, TDK Cashflow, and Halo Brewery.

## Working product thesis

The next generation of brokerage infrastructure will be won by platforms that make complex financial products programmable, compliant, liquid, and easy to embed — without exposing end users to the full complexity underneath.
